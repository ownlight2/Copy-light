Small photo box display update

- Product and category images are now compact boxes across homepage, product list, category list, and detail pages.
- Product cards now use auto-fill grids so more photos fit on one screen.
- Product detail clicked image is constrained to a smaller gallery box.
- Website reads CMS display settings from settings.display and applies CSS variables.
- CMS can control photo heights, detail width, thumbnail height, card minimum widths, and crop style.
